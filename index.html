Contacting me is pretty easy. You can find me on twitter, or just shoot me an email at rrichrs@gmail.com

My name is Ryan Rich. I am a product designer and front-end web developer specializing in rapid prototyping, interaction design, and interface design. Some would say my approach to design is somewhat minimalist. I have a strong background in traditional graphic design as well as extensive experience with web design.

Most of what I do know is design HTML5 prototypes for various products that run in the browser. When I'm not designing or programming I like to listen to music, rock climb, play guitar and go to concerts.
